GAP Insurance

Following a total-loss claim (write-off) our 5-Star Defaqto Return To Invoice/Finance GAP Insurance pays the difference between your motor insurer's settlement figure and either the vehicle's original purchase price or any outstanding finance amount (whichever is greater).

To qualify for cover, you'll need to have purchased your car within the last 100 days and it must be under 10 years old with fewer than 100,000 miles on the clock.

RTI/Finance GAP starts at just £147 and we can cover vehicles valued upto £180,000.
